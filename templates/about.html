About Trung Tran's Passion Project

Trung Tran has been a small business owner who has been running his own companies for more than 10 years. He has gained many valuable skills such as planning, teamwork, and time management, which have helped him to become a successful entrepreneur. Despite his success, he has always had a passion for technology, especially in the area of data engineering and data science. This led him to enroll in Zip Code Wilmington to become a data engineer.

One of Trung's special interests is soccer, which he has been involved in since he was very young. While he no longer plays the sport, he still follows all the major events and would love to work on more projects related to soccer in the future. However, he found that available data on soccer was very limited and often costly, and API calls did not provide him with enough data. Luckily, he discovered some useful datasets on FIFA and Kaggle.

Trung's passion for soccer and data science led him to embark on a passion project. He wanted to use his skills to show what he could do and to explore his interest in soccer in more depth. He decided to create a web application that would predict a player's market value based on various factors such as age, position, and performance. To do this, he used Python, Pandas, PySpark, Matplotlib, and Seaborn to process the data and fit the prediction models.

One of the unique features of Trung's project is that he was able to use AWS services to create a cloud-based application. He recognized that the world is moving towards cloud computing, and companies are starting to migrate to serverless architectures. He saw this as an opportunity to showcase his skills and create a project that was relevant to the current trends in technology. By using AWS services, he was able to make his project more efficient and cost-effective.

On the front-end, Trung created a website to link and visualize all the data. The website provides information about his project, including the data sources and methodology used to make the predictions. He also included a little game to help people learn more about the dataset and how he processed the data.

Trung's passion project is not just about his love for soccer; it is also about demonstrating his skills and showcasing what he can do. His project is a testament to his dedication to learning new things and his willingness to explore his interests in more depth. Trung's project is a great example of how technology can be used to explore and analyze data, and how passion can drive innovation.
